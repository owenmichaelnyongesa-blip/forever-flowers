Flower Care

Keep Your Flowers Fresh

💧
Change Water Daily
Replace the water every day and add a little sugar to keep blooms vibrant longer.
✂
Trim the Stems
Cut 1–2 cm off stems at an angle every 2 days for better water absorption.
🌡
Keep Cool
Avoid direct sunlight and heat. A cool spot extends freshness significantly.
☀
Avoid Sunlight
Direct sun will fade colours. Display in a bright but shaded spot.
💨
Dust Gently
Use a soft brush or cool hair dryer setting to remove dust periodically.
🚫
Avoid Moisture
Keep away from bathrooms or kitchens where humidity causes mould.
🚫
No Water Needed
Preserved roses require no water — simply display and enjoy for months.
🧤
Handle with Care
Avoid touching petals often — natural oils from hands affect preservation.
📦
Store in the Box
When not displaying, keep in original box away from sunlight and humidity.
