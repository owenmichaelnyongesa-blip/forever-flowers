Flower Care

Keep Your Flowers Fresh

💧
Change Water Daily
Replace the water every day and add a little sugar or flower food to keep blooms vibrant longer.
✂️
Trim the Stems
Cut 1–2 cm off the stems at an angle every 2 days. This helps them absorb water better.
🌡️
Keep Cool
Avoid direct sunlight and heat. A cool spot away from fruit bowls extends freshness significantly.
☀️
Keep Away from Sunlight
Direct sun will fade the colours. Display in a bright but shaded spot for the best results.
💨
Dust Gently
Use a soft brush or low-pressure hair dryer on cool setting to remove dust periodically.
🚫
Avoid Moisture
Keep dried flowers away from bathrooms or kitchens where humidity can cause mould.
🚫
No Water Needed
Preserved roses are treated and require no water at all. Simply display and enjoy for months.
🧤
Handle with Care
Avoid touching the petals often. The natural oils from hands can affect the preservation.
📦
Keep in the Box
When not displaying, store in the original box away from direct sunlight and humidity.
